double FreedomOfFormFoundation.AnatomyEngine.Geometry.IRaytraceableSurface.RayIntersect ( Ray  ray)

Intersects the surface with a ray \(\vec{p} + t \vec{s}\), with \(t \geq 0\), returning the minimum \(t\) for which there is an intersection. \(\vec{p}\) is given by ray.StartPosition, and \(\vec{s}\) is given by ray.Direction.
